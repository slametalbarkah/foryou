Pouria Gholami

Pouria Gholami (Persian: پوریا غلامی; born September 20, 1994) is an Iranian footballer who plays as a defender for Iranian club Sanat Mes Kerman in the Azadegan League.





== Club career ==

Gholami has played for Khaybar Khorramabad, Khoshe Talaii, and Malavan Bandar Anzali.
