Habashi, Hamadan

Habashi (Persian: حبشي, also Romanized as Ḩabashī) is a village in Pirsalman Rural District, in the Central District of Asadabad County, Hamadan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 649, in 171 families.
