Hamid Jokar

Hamid Jokar (Persian: حمید جوکار; born Match 26, 1987) is an Iranian football forward who currently plays for Fajr Sepasi.





== Career ==

Jokar started his career with Fajr Sepasi in 2010. He moved to Gostaresh Foolad in the summer of 2014.
