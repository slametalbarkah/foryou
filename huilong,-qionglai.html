Huilong, Qionglai

Huilong (simplified Chinese: 回龙; traditional Chinese: 回龍; pinyin: Huílóng) is a town under the administration of Qionglai City in central Sichuan province, China, situated 24 kilometres (15 mi) southeast of downtown Qionglai and more than twice that southwest of Chengdu. As of 2011, it has two residential communities (社区) and seven villages under its administration.
