Adil Abdullah

Adil Abdullah (Arabic: عَادِل عَبْد الله; 1 January 1923 – 1 July 1986) was an Iraqi with Turkish origin international footballer who played as a forward.

He earned five caps and scored five goals for Iraq, with all five goals coming during 1960 Olympics qualification against Lebanon.
