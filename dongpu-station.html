Dongpu station

Dongpu Station (simplified Chinese: 东圃站; traditional Chinese: 東圃站; Cantonese Yale: Dūngpóu Jaahm) is a station on Line 5 of the Guangzhou Metro. It is located underground Huangpu Avenue East (黄埔大道东) to the west of the Guangzhou Ring Road Expressway (广州环城高速公路) in the Tianhe District. It opened on 28 December 2009.
