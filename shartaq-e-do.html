Shartaq-e Do

Shartaq-e Do (Persian: شرطاق دو, also Romanized as Shartāq Do and Shartāq-e Do; also known as Sharţāq) is a village in Jahad Rural District, Hamidiyeh District, Ahvaz County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 250, in 42 families.
