Mehdi Salehpour

Mehdi Salehpour (Persian: مهدی صالح‌پور, born August 25, 1975, in Tehran) is an Iranian footballer. He has played for both Esteghlal and Persepolis. He won 2001–02 Hazfi Cup with Esteghlal and played for Persepolis in 2004–05 Iran Pro League.

He also plays for "Honarmandan Team", a team consisted of Iranian actors that plays in Charity matches.
