Erik Šuľa

Erik Šuľa (born 30 May 1995) is a Slovak professional footballer who plays for Scottish League Two club Stirling Albion as a defender.





== Club career ==





=== FC Nitra ===

Šuľa made his Fortuna Liga debut for Nitra against ViOn Zlaté Moravce on 17 October 2020.





=== Clyde ===

Šuľa signed for Scottish League One club Clyde in August 2022.
