Subenrat Insaeng

Subenrat Insaeng (Thai: สุเบญรัตน์ อินแสง; born 10 February 1994) is a Thai athlete specialising in the discus throw. She has won multiple medals on regional level.

Her personal best in the event is 61.97 metres set in Kolin[cze]in 2018. This is the current national record.
