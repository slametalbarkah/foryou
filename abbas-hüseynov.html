Abbas Hüseynov

Abbas İsrafil oğlu Hüseynov (born 13 June 1995) is an Azerbaijani footballer who plays as a defender for Qarabağ FK and the Azerbaijan national team.

=== International ===

On 30 January 2018, Hüseynov made his senior international debut for Azerbaijan friendly game against Moldova.
