Arikantha Mahaphruekpong

Arikantha Mahaphruekpong (Thai: อริย์กันตา มหาพฤกษ์พงศ์), formerly Ramita Mahapruekpong (Thai: รมิตา มหาพฤกษ์พงศ์; was born 23 July 1989 at Bangkok, Thailand), she is a Thai actress, and host. She is currently an undergraduate student at the University of London through its International Programmes. Her sister Keerati Mahaplearkpong is also an actress.
